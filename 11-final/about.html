First, I wanted to be a dog breeder. Then, I wanted to be an interior designer. After that, I wanted to be a photographer. Finally, I wanted to be a graphic designer. I go to Massachusetts College of Art and Design as a Junior in the Graphic Design Program. Drop me a line if you want to ask anything or just say hi! Hope you enjoy the view!

After all of those things, I realized that my love for dogs, photography, and graphic design could be combined into my personal production logo called The Little Fox because we used to call my dog growing up a little fox because of his ears.
