WORK SPACE OVERHAUL

Time to get your creative work space all set. If you follow some of these tips and ideas you can find yourself working faster and better!

To do lists are really valuable for people with ADHD and ADD. Download a to-do list template!

Make sure your workspace is clean and tidy! A dirty space will cause distractions!

Try a sudoku or a physical exercise before starting to do some work. Make sure to time yourself, as to not get distracted!

Make sure to keep lots of sticky notes around so you can leave yourself little notes, and maybe some encouragement, too!

When working, make sure to set a certain amount of time for each project. Set timers so that you know when the timer goes off you have to move on. When you do this, you will not get as lost in time as those with ADHD can. It will also help with understanding how much time certain things will take.

Caffeine is an excellent focusing tool, but can have negative effects, such as hyerpactivity. If you want all the benefits of caffeine, but without the downsides of tea and coffee, try a cafea cruda pill, which is a natural sugar pill that bring caffeine to the brain in a more natural way.

If you're feeling stuck, talk to friends and family for support. Try and help them to understand what it is that you are going through.

Make sure to always write down things as people are talking to you. Or try using recordings and you can play them back later if you find yourself forgetting things!

Keeping detailed notes is always important! Make sure to keep a little notebook or sketchbook with you.

Organizing your things so they don't get lost is very important. Make sure to sort your tools so they will not get misplaced! Try looking at some interesting ways that people organize on Pinterest, because they have some very creative ideas.

Make sure keep a visual display of your weekly plan so that you can always see what your week is going to entail. This can help you from taking on too much work. If you take on too much work and you don't realize your schedule, you could get caught making mistakes.

Keeping a monthly calendar has the same benefits as keeping a weekly calendar: Visualizing time. Make sure to keep one of these so that you and others can see what you have for the whole month. Things can get lost if you don't visualize them.

A planner is one of the most important things that you could use. It keeps all of your dates and times visualized, as well as stores all your important notes and information. This planner also doubles as a CBT diary for you to keept track of your CBT away from the therapists office.
