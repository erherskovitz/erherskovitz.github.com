My project is going to be an interactive site about ADD and ADHD. It will be a site that will educate those who do not have ADD/ADHD and help people who do have it. Each item will symbolize a certain kind of information and will signal which kind of information will appear when you hover/click on the images.

When you hover on the elements, it will animate and then when you click it will change to a greyed out background. Then some text will appear to go along with the image that person has selected so that they can learn a bit about ADD. The information will also contain links, gifs and possibly videos for more information.
