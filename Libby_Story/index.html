the Story of Libby

There was a girl, so odd and quirky.
She had all these ideas, but her future was murky.

So she tried all different niches.
From dog walking, to baby sitting, even making Quiches.

Then she tried interior design
But the reality for her seemed slightly benign.

On one fateful day,
Her friend Kimi would say

"I took this funny photo, but it's kinda a flop,
Why don't we try to edit it in photoshop!"

And so the love affair begun
Libby took so many classes, at first just for fun.

"High school is ending soon, you know"
Her father said, with an anticipated glow

"What ever shall you do with your future and career?"
"How about art school," she said with some fear.

Her parents, to her surprise,
Were completely behind her, they gave her high fives.

So the search to find the perfect school
Maybe RISD or pratt, but she wasn't a fool.

The tour of MassArt sealed the deal
No where else would be any better, she could feel.

So she worked as hard as she could and applied
Then the acceptance came in the mail, she nearly cried.

Off she went to fulfill her dream,
Where she found school might not be all it would seem.
Hard work and long days, exhaustion ensued.
Libby and her work would sometimes feud.

Then she decided to take a class like one she had done before...
Web design, she found, would be something that she would adore.
So her love for design and art returned.
Her creativity once again burned.

So here we are, with Libby all aglow
Feel free to message her if there is anything you wish to know!

Hope you enjoyed this tale,
But there shall be more to come, without fail.
